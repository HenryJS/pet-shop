RESERVE YOUR PUP!

Thank you for your interests in our pups. We are currently accepting reservations on all our puppys which gives you the option to book a newly born puppy before its offered on sale to anyone else.

All our pupies are fully vaccinated and dewormed. A detailed veterinary clinic booklet will be provided for each puppy for easy monitoring of the puppy's health

How does our reservation work?

  • Let us know which breed you want
  • Make a 15% reservation deposit
  • When reserving a puppy, the payment plan are as follows
  • 15% on reservation
  • 15% 2weeks reservation
  • Balance 50% on delivery
  • Collect your puppy from Elgon View Eldoret or delivery can be organised
